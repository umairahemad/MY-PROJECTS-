Exercise Counter Demo

How to Use:
  • Choose your exercise: Squat, Situp, Bicep Curl, Pushup, Jumping Jack.
  • Click Add Rep each time you complete a rep (for demo).
  • Click Reset to reset the counter.
  • Switch exercises anytime using the buttons below.

Rules for Counting Reps

  1. Full Range of Motion: Each rep must reach the minimum required movement (e.g., squat depth, curl height).
  2. No Cheating: Reps done too quickly or with insufficient range will be marked as "cheat reps" and not counted as correct.
  3. Switching Exercises: You can switch exercises anytime; counters reset for each exercise.
  4. Metrics: The app shows correct reps, cheat reps, and live warnings for cheating.
  5. Instructions: All controls and feedback are shown on the webcam feed for clarity.
